About the studio

An independent game studio building strategy, culture, and legacy into play.

Da Mystro Gamings is an independent game studio based in Silver Spring, Maryland, dedicated to creating strategy, educational, and culturally inspired games that bring people together.

What we believe

Games can do more than entertain.

They can teach, challenge, preserve culture, and create meaningful experiences for players around the world. Founded by Yohanes Hailegeberel, Da Mystro Gamings was created with a simple vision: build original games that combine strategic gameplay with authentic storytelling and long-term innovation.

Our work

From competitive cards to educational play and original worlds.

Our projects range from competitive card games and educational experiences to original books and interactive worlds inspired by Ethiopian history and culture. Although we are an early-stage studio, our goal reaches far beyond launching a single title. We are building a lasting entertainment company that develops games, educational experiences, and original intellectual property for players worldwide.

Current portfolio

Four projects, four paths into the same studio vision.

Bank Co

A strategy card game built around probability, decision-making, and risk management.

Da Conquer

A strategic rummy-inspired game rooted in Ethiopian tradition and modern gameplay.

Injera Be Wote

An educational game designed to make learning mathematics engaging and fun.

Fall of Abyssinia

An original story universe that expands our creative vision beyond games.

Our core values

Every project is guided by what the studio stands for.

01

Creativity

Original ideas matter, especially when they come from real life, family, culture, and imagination.

02

Integrity

The studio is being built carefully, honestly, and with respect for players and partners.

03

Strategic Thinking

Games should reward decisions, patience, timing, pattern reading, and smart risk.

04

Cultural Authenticity

Cultural inspiration should connect to the product's story, structure, and reason for existing.

05

Continuous Learning

We improve through testing, feedback, research, mentorship, and disciplined iteration.

06

Community

Games are stronger when they bring players, families, educators, and builders together.
