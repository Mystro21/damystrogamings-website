Games and IP

Current projects across strategy, culture, education, and original worlds.

Da Mystro Gamings is building publicly and carefully. The projects below reflect current development status and should not be treated as released products unless future updates say otherwise.

In Development

Priority launch product

Bank Co

Bank Co is a fast-paced strategic card game built around probability, risk, and decision-making. A player receives two cards and decides whether a third card will fall between their ranks.

  • Focused on risk, probability, and quick decisions.
  • Designed to be simple to understand but strategic to play.
  • Not currently listed as released or available for download.

Competitive card strategy

Da Conquer

Da Conquer is an Ethiopian-inspired rummy-style strategy card game shaped by family culture, sets, runs, tactical card management, and a 41-point opening system.

  • Currently playable and actively being tested.
  • Da SayKa is the beginner/training mode inside Da Conquer.
  • Not currently presented as a publicly released game.

Prototype

Educational strategy

Injera Be Wote

Injera Be Wote is an educational math and strategy card game concept where players capture cards by matching numbers or using addition to create the value of a selected card.

  • A Unity gameplay prototype has been developed.
  • Built around capture, matching, and addition mechanics.
  • The larger school-focused product is still being developed.
