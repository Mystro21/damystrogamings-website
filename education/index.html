Education

Learning through real gameplay, not worksheets wearing costumes.

Da Mystro Gamings is interested in educational products that help children practice math, strategy, and decision-making through play. Injera Be Wote is the current prototype leading this direction.

Prototype

Injera Be Wote

Math and strategy through capture, matching, and addition.

Injera Be Wote is an educational math and strategy card game concept. Players capture cards by matching a number or using addition to create the value of a selected card.

  • A Unity gameplay prototype has been developed.
  • The educational and school-focused product is still being developed.
  • The goal is genuine gameplay that reinforces mathematical thinking.

School and educational collaboration

For educators, families, youth programs, and school partners.

Da Mystro Gamings is open to conversations about educational opportunities and school collaboration. We are not claiming existing school contracts or formal school partnerships; this page creates a clear path for serious conversations as the product develops.
