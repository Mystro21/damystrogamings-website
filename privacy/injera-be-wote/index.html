Injera Be Wote Privacy

Privacy information for Injera Be Wote

Injera Be Wote is currently a prototype. Last updated: July 8, 2026.

Current status

A Unity gameplay prototype has been developed around core capture, matching, and addition mechanics. The larger educational and school-focused product is still being developed.

Current data practices

Based on the current public information provided for this website, Da Mystro Gamings has not confirmed Injera Be Wote player accounts, advertising, marketing mailing lists, website analytics, cloud saves, or gameplay tracking for a public release. If any of those systems are added to Injera Be Wote, especially for a children or school-focused release, this page must be updated before submission or release.

Support contact

For privacy questions, access requests, deletion requests, education inquiries, or Injera Be Wote support, contact damystrogamings21@gmail.com.
