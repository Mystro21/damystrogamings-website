Privacy

Privacy Policy for Da Mystro Gamings

This Privacy Policy explains how Da Mystro Gamings LLC handles information for the public company website at damystrogamings.com. Last updated: July 8, 2026.

Who we are

Da Mystro Gamings LLC is an emerging game studio based in Silver Spring, Maryland, USA. You can contact us at damystrogamings21@gmail.com.

Company website data

The public company website uses a minimal-data approach. The website does not provide user accounts, player login, gameplay tracking, advertising, marketing mailing lists, or analytics tools. The website does not intentionally set unnecessary cookies.

Email contact

If you choose to contact Da Mystro Gamings LLC by email, the company receives the information you include in that email, such as your email address, name if provided, message content, and any device or issue details you choose to include. We use that information to read, respond to, and manage your request.

Hosting and technical information

Like most websites, the hosting provider may process basic technical information needed to deliver the website, protect the service, and maintain reliability. This may include information such as IP address, browser type, requested page, date and time of access, and basic security logs. Da Mystro Gamings does not use this website to build advertising profiles.

How information is shared

We do not sell personal information. Information may be handled by service providers that help operate the website or email systems, or disclosed if required by law, safety, security, or legal process.

Retention and deletion

Email messages may be retained as needed to respond to requests, manage company records, and protect the company or users. You can contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of information you sent to us by email. We may need to retain limited information where required for legal, security, or business record purposes.

Children

The public company website is not designed to collect personal information from children. If a parent, guardian, school, or educator believes a child has sent personal information to Da Mystro Gamings, contact us so we can review and respond.

Games and apps

Individual Da Mystro games and apps may have separate privacy practices depending on the actual technology used by each product. Game-specific privacy pages are available for current projects: Bank Co, Da Conquer, and Injera Be Wote.

Contact

For privacy questions, support requests, or deletion requests, contact damystrogamings21@gmail.com.
