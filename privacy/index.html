Privacy

Privacy information for the Da Mystro Gamings website

This page is the privacy architecture for the public company website at damystrogamings.com. It must receive final company and legal review before public launch.

Company website data

The initial Da Mystro Gamings company website is being built with a minimal-data approach. The public website is not intended to provide player accounts, user login, gameplay tracking, advertising, marketing mailing lists, or unnecessary cookies.

Email contact

If you choose to contact Da Mystro Gamings LLC by email, the company will receive the information you include in that email. Please avoid sending passwords, payment details, or sensitive personal information by email.

Games and apps

Individual Da Mystro games and apps may have their own privacy practices in the future, depending on the actual technology used by each product. Game-specific privacy information should be published when those products use systems such as player accounts, cloud saves, analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or third-party services.

Contact
